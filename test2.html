Method of statement minimizing Waste Density:

  1. Compaction: Compaction is the most common method of reducing solid waste density in landfills. It is done using heavy machinery to press down on the waste, which forces out the air and reduces the volume,for more details see that ghraph as shown in the picture below ⏬ ⏬
  2. Waste separation: Waste separation can also help to reduce solid waste density. This is because different types of waste have different densities. For example, paper is less dense than metal. By separating waste into different streams, it is possible to compact each stream more effectively, which reduces the overall density of the waste.
